=== ENGLISH ===
App name: SallesJam Compliance
Short description: AI compliance assistant for Gmail. Check emails for LGPD, NR-1 and Brazilian regulations.
Detailed description:
SallesJam Compliance brings AI-powered regulatory analysis directly to your Gmail inbox.

Features:
• Analyze emails for LGPD (Brazilian data privacy law) compliance
• Detect personal data exposure risks in email conversations
• Check labor compliance requirements (NR-1)
• Get instant AI responses about Brazilian regulations
• Multi-market support: Brazil, US, Mexico, Colombia, Argentina

How it works:
1. Open any email in Gmail
2. Click the SallesJam icon in the add-on panel
3. The AI analyzes the email for compliance risks
4. Get instant recommendations

Privacy: No email content is stored. All processing is done via encrypted API calls to SallesJam servers.
