=== G2 — EcoSystem AEC + Regulatory ===

Nome do produto: EcoSystem AEC + Regulatory
Categoria G2: Compliance Software / GRC (Governance, Risk & Compliance) Platforms
Empresa: Global Match Engenharia

Tagline (curta):
86 AI compliance agents — from SOC2 to NR-1 to EU AI Act — in one platform.

Descrição curta:
AI-powered compliance and regulatory platform with 86 specialized agents
covering global standards (SOC2, ISO27001, NIS2, DORA, CSRD, EU AI Act) and
Brazil-specific regulations (NR-1, LGPD, CBS/IBS). Each agent scores against
real regulatory catalogs — not generic LLM output — and produces
audit-ready reports.

Descrição detalhada:
EcoSystem AEC + Regulatory replaces manual compliance work with AI agents
built on deterministic, real-catalog rigor: LLMs extract facts, a fixed
regulatory catalog does the scoring — so results are auditable, not just
"AI-generated."

Coverage includes:
• European Compliance: CSRD, DORA, SOC2, ISO27001, NIS2
• Enterprise Risk: Vendor Risk Assessment, Contract Risk Analysis,
  M&A Due Diligence, Board ESG Reporting
• Brazil Regulatory: NR-1 (psychosocial risk), LGPD, CBS/IBS tax reform
• Global: EU AI Act (Art. 5, Annex III, Art. 50), Regulatory Monitor
  across 50+ jurisdictions
• Governance: Whistleblower case management

Pricing: subscription plans from $99 to $4,999/month depending on scope
(single-agent to full-suite). Free diagnostic available on select plans.

Integrations: Stripe billing, Zapier/Make.com, HubSpot CRM.

Principais recursos p/ preencher nos campos do G2:
- Deterministic compliance scoring (not black-box LLM classification)
- Audit-ready PDF/report generation
- Multi-jurisdiction support (BR, US, EU, MX, CO, AR)
- 48h turnaround on assessments
- Subscription model, self-serve checkout
