## 1.0.0

Initial release to public.

1. New trigger! `new_call_completed` -- fires when a call to your AION Voice Receptionist number finishes.
2. New trigger! `new_lead_captured` -- fires when the AI receptionist captures a caller's name and contact info as a lead.
3. New search! `find_subscription` -- looks up your AION Voice Receptionist subscription status by email.
