## 4. Product Hunt — Launch Strategy

### When to launch
- **Best day:** Tuesday or Wednesday (highest engagement)
- **Time:** 00:01 PT (7:00 UTC) — start of the voting day
- **Avoid:** weekends, holidays, days with major launches (OpenAI, Google)

### What to launch
Do NOT launch the whole platform at once. Pick the **strongest single Copilot**:

**Product: "AION — AI Compliance Copilot for the EU AI Act"**
- Focused on the EU AI Act (Article 50 transparency deadline: 2 August 2026)
- Single, well-defined product, easy to understand
- If it lands Top 5 of the day, cross-sell the rest of the Copilot platform
